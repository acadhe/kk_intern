General Script: About Urban Social Forum
Concept:
Urban Social Forum is an inclusive platform to discuss, share and promote ideas, practices, and knowledge. It is a space to develop networks, build visibility for citizen initiatives, and create discourse with partners from across Indonesia and Asia. The 4th Urban Social Forum under the tagline ‘Another City is Possible’ is an event that supports the idea of the need for new and alternative models of urban development that promote social justice, inclusiveness, and citizen participation.

Last editions
The first Urban Social Forum in 2013 was held in Solo and brought together 120 participants. The second Forum in 2014 was held in the same city, the event grew and gathered around 300 participants from all over Indonesia. The 3rd Urban Social Forum was held in Surabaya and gathered more than 1,000 participants from 22 cities in Indonesia and 10 countries from around the world. The growing popularity for the Urban Social Forum can be attributed to a growing awareness of the need for civil society to debate and collaboration to develop and promote solutions for today’s complex urban problems. Kota Kita initiated the Urban Social Forum in 2013 and ever since, has been supported by voluntary contributions from individuals and partners from all over Indonesia
